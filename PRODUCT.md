# Hidden Harvest — Seeds Report

## Product Purpose
Seeds Report is an AI-generated HTML presentation delivered as an email attachment. It presents automation opportunities found in a company's operations. The recipient (a business owner or manager) opens it in their browser to review what Hidden Harvest found during the discovery phase.

## Users
Business owners and managers at Dutch SMEs who have submitted a form describing their manual processes. They are fellow entrepreneurs — not tech-savvy necessarily, but pragmatic and time-pressed. They want clarity, not jargon.

## Brand
Hidden Harvest — "We find the growth hiding in your operations." An AI automation agency based in Delft. Farm metaphors as wrapper; direct technical clarity underneath. Tone: a farmer who happens to build automation. Peer-to-peer, grounded, short sentences.

## Register
brand

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboards (white + blue, card grids)
- Corporate consulting reports (navy + gold, formal headers)
- AI/tech startup templates (gradient text, glassmorphism, neon)
- PowerPoint defaults (clip art, bullet walls)

## Strategic principles
- First impression must feel premium and hand-crafted, not AI-generated
- Every number should feel earned, not inflated
- Farm identity is visual texture, not costume
- The report is an invitation to a conversation, not a closing argument
